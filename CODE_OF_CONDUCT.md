# Code of Conduct

**In the name of Allah, the Most Gracious, the Most Merciful.**

## Our Pledge

Badal Technologies and the Iqra AI project were built on the foundation of Islamic morals and ethics. For us, there is no separation between personal integrity, business conduct, and political stance; they are one and the same.

We are not just building software; we are building the **Leaders of Tomorrow**. We believe that intellectual, financial, influential, and political capital must be accessible to all who strive for justice and excellence.

Therefore, we pledge to make participation in our project and our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity (male or female), level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, or religion—**provided they align with the ethical and moral standards set forth in this document.**

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to creating a positive environment include:

*   **Ihsan (Excellence):** Striving for the highest quality in code, documentation, and communication.
*   **Adab (Respect & Etiquette):** Treating others with dignity, patience, and kindness, even during disagreements.
*   **Amanah (Trustworthiness):** Being honest, transparent, and honoring commitments.
*   **Nasiha (Sincere Advice):** Giving and accepting constructive feedback gracefully for the betterment of the project and the individual.
*   **Empowerment:** Helping others grow their intellectual and financial capital. We encourage mentorship and knowledge sharing.

## Unacceptable Behavior

We hold a strict zero-tolerance policy towards behavior that contradicts our values. Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

*   **Support for Oppression:** Advocating for, minimizing, or financially/politically supporting regimes or entities involved in occupation, genocide, or the oppression of Muslim nations and other oppressed peoples.
*   **Anti-Islamic Sentiment:** Any form of Islamophobia, mockery of religious values, or promotion of ideologies hostile to Islam.
*   **Harassment:** Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks.
*   **Dishonesty:** Plagiarism, fraud, or using the software for deceptive or illegal purposes (scams, deepfakes, harm).
*   **Sexualized Conduct:** The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances.

## Political & Ethical Stance

We do not believe in "neutrality" when it comes to justice.

Badal Technologies is a mission-driven organization based in the Sultanate of Oman. We openly declare our stance against oppression and injustice globally. We expect all contributors and users of this project to respect this stance.

While we welcome contributors from all backgrounds, we will not tolerate the use of our platform or community channels to promote narratives that support the state of Israel, Zionism, or any other entity currently engaged in the oppression of Muslims or humanity at large. Violating this clause is grounds for immediate ban and license revocation.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces (GitHub, Discord, Social Media), and it also applies when an individual is representing the project or its community in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting the project team at **security@iqra.bot**.

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated and will result in a response that is deemed necessary and appropriate to the circumstances. The project team is obligated to maintain confidentiality with regard to the reporter of an incident.

**Badal Technologies / Abdullah bin Amir Trading SPC** reserves the right to:
1.  Remove comments or code that violate this Code of Conduct.
2.  Ban any contributor or user temporarily or permanently.
3.  Revoke the software license of any entity found to be in violation of the ethical restrictions defined here and in the `LICENSE.md` file.
